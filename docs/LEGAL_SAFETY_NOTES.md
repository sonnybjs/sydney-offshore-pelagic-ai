# Legal And Safety Notes

Fishing rules can change. Users must check official NSW DPI sources for current regulations, closures, bag limits, possession limits and protected species information.

Marine conditions can be dangerous. This app is not navigation advice, marine safety advice, weather advice or legal advice.

The app should not encourage overfishing or unsafe offshore travel. Users should follow responsible fishing practices, handle fish carefully, respect protected species and consider catch-and-release where appropriate.

Southern Bluefin Tuna can have stricter limits and reporting obligations. Users must check official current rules before fishing for or keeping Southern Bluefin Tuna.

All demo coordinates and POIs are approximate and not verified fishing marks.
